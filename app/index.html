Kailash Mansarovar Yatra 2026

A 13-day Hindu pilgrimage to Mt Kailash and Lake Manasarovar across the Tibetan Plateau. 7 July 2026 (Mumbai depart) to 19 July 2026 (Mumbai return).

Route

Mumbai (BOM) -> Kathmandu (KTM, 2 nights) -> Lhasa (LXA, 2 nights) -> Ali (NGQ) -> Lake Manasarovar (Chiu Gompa shore) -> Darchen -> Kailash parikrama (3-day clockwise trek, 52 km) -> Darchen -> Ali -> Lhasa -> Kathmandu -> Mumbai.

High point: Dolma La pass at 5,630 m / 18,471 ft on Day 8. Longest trek day: 22 km on Day 8.

Day-by-day

  1. Day 1 (Tue 7 Jul) Kathmandu: Arrival window at KTM airport. Marriott. 1,380 m.
  2. Day 2 (Wed 8 Jul) Kathmandu: Indian Embassy NOC visit, Pashupatinath darshan. 1,380 m.
  3. Day 3 (Thu 9 Jul) Lhasa: Fly KTM-LXA. First altitude jump to 3,656 m.
  4. Day 4 (Fri 10 Jul) Lhasa: Acclimatization. Jokhang, Barkhor, Potala. 3,656 m.
  5. Day 5 (Sat 11 Jul) Mansarovar: Fly LXA-Ali, drive to Lake Manasarovar. Sleep 4,670 m.
  6. Day 6 (Sun 12 Jul) Mansarovar: Holy snan (bath) at the lake, Chiu Gompa visit. 4,670 m.
  7. Day 7 (Mon 13 Jul) Dirapuk: Drive to Darchen, Yamadwar gate, trek up Lha Chu valley to Dirapuk camp on Kailash's north face. ~18 km. Sleep 4,890 m.
  8. Day 8 (Tue 14 Jul) Dolma La crossing: THE day. Steep climb past Shiva Tsal to Dolma La pass at 5,630 m, descend past Gauri Kund to Zuthulphuk. ~22 km, 8 to 9 hours active trek. Sleep 4,760 m.
  9. Day 9 (Wed 15 Jul) Darchen: Zuthulphuk to Darchen along Zhong Chu valley, parikrama closes. ~14 km. Drive to overnight stop.
  10. Day 10 (Thu 16 Jul) Lhasa: Drive to Ali, fly Ali-LXA. Return to St Regis Lhasa.
  11. Day 11 (Fri 17 Jul) Kathmandu: Fly LXA-KTM.
  12. Day 12 (Sat 18 Jul) Kathmandu: Buffer / rest day in KTM.
  13. Day 13 (Sun 19 Jul) Mumbai: Fly KTM-BOM. Yatra closes.

Key facts

  • Group: 23 yatris from 4 origin cities (Mumbai, Dubai, Port Louis, New York).
  • Parikrama: 52 km clockwise circuit around Mt Kailash, completed over Days 7-9.
  • Altitude profile: sea level -> 1,380 m (KTM) -> 3,656 m (Lhasa) -> 4,670 m (Mansarovar) -> 5,630 m (Dolma La).
  • Connectivity: WiFi + phone at hotels in Kathmandu and Lhasa (some apps blocked in Tibet, VPN app needed for WhatsApp). Phone only at Mansarovar. No phone or WiFi during the 3-day parikrama; Sherpa team carries comms.
  • Coldest expected: -5 to +5 C overnight at Dolma La. Warmest: 29 C in Kathmandu monsoon.
