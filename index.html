Critical notes

Day 8 (Dolma La) is the singular hardest day. 22 km on foot with no horses on the ascent. Whiteout possible at the pass. Start at 4 AM. UV at 5,630 m is off scale and snow doubles the dose. Both Cat 4 sunglasses on body. Hand warmers x4.
Tibet is behind the Great Firewall. WhatsApp, Google, Instagram all blocked without a VPN. Holafly China eSIM + Astrill VPN tested from India by 4 July.
Suitcase stays in Lhasa for 5 nights (Days 5 to 10). Anything needed on the trek lives in the YPO duffle or the personal daypack. Keep a clean reserve outfit + spare power bank + meds in the suitcase as lost bag insurance.
Diamox starts ~4 July (3 days before KTM arrival). Get the prescription from Dr. Hingorani by 30 June.

Yatra coordinated through YPO. Travel partner contact via Isra. Emergency tracking via this page.
